Exoplanets Information

1. Proxima Centauri b

Proxima Centauri b is the closest exoplanet to Earth. It orbits in the habitable zone of the star Proxima Centauri, which may allow it to support liquid water. Scientists are still studying its atmosphere and surface conditions.

Mass: 1.17 Earth masses

Distance from Earth: 4.24 light years

2. Ross 128 b

Ross 128 b is located 11 light years away from Earth and is one of the closest potenrially habitable planets. It orbits a red dwarf star and could have a surface temperature that allows for liquid water.

Mass: 1.35 Earth masses

Distance from Earth: 11 light years

3. HD 85512 b

HD 85512 b is an exoplanet located around 36 light years away from Earth. It orbits its star in the habitable zone, making it another cadidate for potential life.

Mass: 3.6 Earth masses

Distance from Earth: 36 light years

4. Gliese 677Cc

Gliese 677Cc is a super-Earth that orbits in the habitable zone of the red dwarf star Gliese 677Cc. It is a strong candidate for habitability due to its location and size.

mASS: 4.5 Earth masses

Distance from Earth: 23.62 light years

5. Kepler-186f

Kepler-186f is the first Earth-sized planet discoreved in the habitable zone of its star. It is located about 500 light years away in the Kepler-186 system and could potentially have conditions similar to Earth.

Mass: 1.0 Earth masses

Distance from Earth: 500 light years
